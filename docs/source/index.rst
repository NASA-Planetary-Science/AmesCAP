Welcome to AmesCAP's Documentation!
===================================
**AmesCAP** is a Python library with executables that enable command-line interaction with netCDF files output by the `**Mars Global Climate Model (MGCM)** <https://github.com/nasa/AmesGCM>`_. The MGCM and AmesCAP are developed and maintained by the `**Mars Climate Modeling Center (MCMC)** <https://www.nasa.gov/mars-climate-modeling-center-ames>`_ at NASA's Ames Research Center in Mountain View, CA.
